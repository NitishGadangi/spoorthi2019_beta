Spoorthi

Spoorthi is the annual techno-cultural symposium conducted by the Department of Electronics and Communications Engineering, JNTUH CEH. Spoorthi was first started in 2004 as a technical fest and right from its inception, it has received immense response from engineering students from all over the state of Andhra Pradesh owing to the position of JNTUCEH as the topmost engineering college in the state. Spoorthi offers a platform for students to compete with their peers from all over the state in a myriad of events that test their mettle and knowledge in the Electronics and communications engineering domain.

ECE Department

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering was established as a full-fledged entity to offer    B. Tech degree in 1973. With its Qualified, Experienced and committed Faculty/Staff, the department has expanded its activities enormously over the past 39 years. The department offers One UG, Two Integrated and Seven PG programs in various specializations in addition to full-time and part-time Ph. D and M. S. by Research programs. It has well equipped laboratories, its own computer center and departmental library to cater the needs of UG and PG students and research scholars. Many Doctorates and four M. S by Research Degrees were awarded by the department in the past decade.

JNTUH College Of Engineering

JNTUH College of Engineering Hyderabad was established as Nagarujuna Sagar Engineering College in 1965 by the Government of Andhra Pradesh. With the introduction of Jawaharlal Nehru Technology University in 1972, it became a Constituent College of the University and was renamed as JNTUH College of Engineering, Hyderabad. After being located in temporary buildings, the College was shifted to the present 100- acre site in Kukatpally in 1984. In 2008, after the University was divided into four separate entities by the Ordinance of the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh, the JNTU College of Engineering, Hyderabad was renamed as JNTUH College of Engineering, Hyderabad and retained as a constituent College of JNT University Hyderabad. The JNTUH College of Engineering, Hyderabad has become Autonomous in the year 2001.
